# **App Name**: DOCTIVO

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Symptom Categorization: An AI-powered tool that analyzes patient symptoms and medical history to recommend the most relevant doctor specializations.
- Swift OTP Authentication: Streamlined mobile-first login interface utilizing a simulated OTP verification for secure access.
- Comprehensive Medical Onboarding: A structured two-part registration flow for capturing demographic data, chronic conditions, and allergy histories.
- Filtered Specialist Discovery: A targeted search and category hub enabling users to find doctors based on specific health concerns or local availability.
- Multi-Patient Family Portal: Centralized interface to create and manage profiles for family members, enabling streamlined booking for dependents.
- Dynamic Appointment Scheduler: Interactive booking flow with real-time visual slots and live status tracking (Confirmed, Waiting, In Consultation).
- Local Records Vault: Browser-based persistence of medical history and booking receipts to maintain access without external database reliance.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Trust Blue (#2563EB) chosen to evoke medical professionalism and reliability.
- Background Color: Healthcare White (#F1F5F9) to provide a clean, clinical, and spacious feel.
- Accent Color: Vitality Cyan (#0891B2), an analogous shade that highlights secondary interactive elements with high clarity.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for an objective, machined, and neutral look suitable for complex medical data.
- Adheres to a 9:16 mobile-first vertical layout with rounded cards (#F8FAFC) to compartmentalize health information efficiently.
- Consistent use of humanist health symbols and badges (🩺, ❤️, 🦷) for instant category recognition.
- Smooth status transitions for the Live Sync Badge and micro-interactions on specialist cards to enhance the user's sense of progress.
